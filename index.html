Duniya dekhe par, khamosh kyun rahi?
Palestine ke aansoo, samundar ban gaye

[Verse 3]
Bachhon ki hasi, ab yaadein ban gayi

Kitni kahaniyan, bina anjaam ke
Jeevan ki kitaab, kyun band ho gayi?
[Outro]
Ek baar to sunlo, zara dekhlo
Insaaniyat ke naam pe, kuch to karlo
Palestine ke aansoo, samundar ban gaye
Shanti ki roshni, kabhi to jalegi…
